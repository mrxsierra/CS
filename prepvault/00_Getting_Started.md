## 🔍 The Three Pillars of the 2026-27 Market
1. **The Rise of GCCs**: Global enterprises are building massive innovation centers. They pay product-level salaries but demand high-level execution.
2. **The GenAI Skill Gap**: There is a massive shortage of "GenAI-ready" engineers. If you know how to build *with* and *for* AI, you are in the top 5%.
3. **The Death of CGPA Gates**: Pedigree is being replaced by **Proof of Work** (GitHub history, API deployment sandboxes, and behavioral AI screens).
