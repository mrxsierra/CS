# 90-Day Interview Readiness Plan

This roadmap provides a structured path from "Starting Out" to "Landed Offer."

---

## Month 1: Foundations & Systems (Building the Engine)
- **Weeks 1-2**: Master the 12 Core DSA Patterns. Focus on **Arrays**, **Hashing**, **Two Pointers**, and **Sliding Window**.
- **Weeks 3-4**: Deep dive into **System Design Fundamentals** (Load Balancing, Caching, Databases). Build high-level blueprints for 3 common systems.
- **Milestone**: Complete 50 LeetCode "Medium" problems categorized by pattern.

## Month 2: Role Mastery & Portfolio (Sharpening the Blade)
- **Weeks 5-6**: Complete your specific **Role Track** (e.g., Backend, Frontend). Focus on "Language Internals" and "Stack Deep Dives."
- **Weeks 7-8**: Build or polish your **"Proof of Work"** projects. Ensure your GitHub looks professional and your resume is ATS-optimized.
- **Milestone**: Get your first 3 informational interviews/referral leads.

## Month 3: Interview Execution (Closing the Deal)
- **Weeks 9-10**: Behavioral prep using the **STAR method**. Practice mock interviews (Pramp, interviewing.io).
- **Weeks 11-12**: Full-scale application and interview loop. Focus on technical communication and "Thinking Aloud."
- **Milestone**: Reach final rounds at 2+ target companies.

---

## Daily Routine for 90 Days
- **Morning (90m)**: 1-2 Deep work coding problems (Pattern-based).
- **Afternoon (60m)**: Read 1 System Design or Stack Deep Dive module.
- **Evening (30m)**: Networking/Application (LinkedIn connection, Referral follow-up).
