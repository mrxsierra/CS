# ATS-Optimized Resume Writing

Most large companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes before a human ever sees them. To pass the "bot," your resume needs to be machine-readable and keyword-rich.

---

## 1. Machine-Readable Formatting
- **File Type**: Use PDF (unless explicitly asked for Word).
- **Layout**: Simple, single-column layout is safest. Avoid complex tables, graphics, or sidebars that can confuse the parser.
- **Fonts**: Use standard web-safe fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Roboto.
- **Sections**: Use clear, standard headings (e.g., "Experience," "Education," "Skills").

## 2. Keyword Optimization
The ATS looks for specific keywords from the job description.
- **Skills Section**: List your technical stack clearly (e.g., Python, React, AWS, Docker).
- **Action Verbs**: Use strong action verbs like "Architected," "Implemented," "Optimized," and "Led."
- **Contextual Keywords**: If the job asks for "Distributed Systems," ensure that exact phrase appears in your experience section.

## 3. The Formula for Bullet Points (XYZ Formula)
Google recommends the XYZ formula: "Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]."
- *Weak*: "Worked on a React app to improve performance."
- *Strong*: "Reduced page load time by **35% (Y)** by implementing **React Server Components and code splitting (Z)** for the core dashboard **(X)**."

## 4. Technical Resume Sections
1. **Header**: Name, LinkedIn, Portfolio/GitHub, Email, Phone.
2. **Professional Summary**: 2-3 sentences highlighting your years of experience and top 3 skills.
3. **Skills**: Categorized by Language, Framework, Tools, and Cloud.
4. **Experience**: Reverse chronological.
5. **Projects**: Highlight your "Proof of Work."
6. **Education**: Degree, University, and Graduation Year.

---

## Common Interview Questions
- **"Tell me about a time you optimized a system."**: Have a resume bullet ready to expand on using the STAR method.
- **"Why did you choose this specific tech stack for your project?"**: Be prepared to justify the architectural choices listed on your resume.
