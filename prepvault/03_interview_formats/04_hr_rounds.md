# 🤝 HR Rounds

## Description
Cultural fit and logistics interviews. These rounds cover compensation expectations, team matching, and general logistics. While less technically demanding, they are critical for closing the offer.

## Key Concepts
- **Compensation**: Know your market rate. Be prepared to discuss salary, equity, and signing bonuses.
- **Company Values**: Show genuine interest in the company's mission and culture.
- **Team Matching**: Ask about team structure, tech stack, and growth opportunities.
- **Timeline**: Understand the hiring process timeline, next steps, and decision dates.

## Tips
- Have 3-4 thoughtful questions prepared about the team, culture, and growth.
- Be honest about your experience level and what you're looking for.
- Negotiate respectfully — use market data (Blind, Levels.fyi, Glassdoor) as leverage.
