# 🧠 Behavioral Rounds

## Description
Soft skills and experience-based interviews. These rounds test cultural fit, leadership, collaboration, and your ability to learn from failures. At companies like Amazon, behavioral rounds carry as much weight as technical rounds.

## Key Concepts
- **STAR Method**: Situation, Task, Action, Result — the gold standard framework for structuring answers.
- **Leadership Principles**: Every company has values (e.g., Amazon's 16 LPs). Map your stories to these.
- **Conflict Resolution**: How you handle disagreements with teammates, PMs, or stakeholders.
- **Failure & Learning**: Be honest about a real failure and show what you learned.

## Common Questions
1. "Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a teammate."
2. "Describe a challenging technical problem you solved."
3. "Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned."
4. "Why do you want to work here?"
5. "Describe a time you took initiative beyond your role."
